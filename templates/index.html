2. Shaking Level

Shaking level explanation:

Shake Level >=50 >=100 >=500 >=1000 >=2000 >=3000
Explanation Insensible shaking. Nobody will feel it. Moderate shaking. Almost all the people will feel it. Strong shaking. People will have difficulty to do things. Very strong shaking. People will have difficulty to stand. Extremely strong shaking. People will have difficulty to crawl on the ground. Catastrophic shaking. The wooden houses will crack.
