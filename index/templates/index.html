2. Shaking Level

Shaking level explanation:

Shake Level Explanation
>=50 Insensible shaking. Nobody will feel it.
>=100 Moderate shaking. Almost all the people will feel it.
>=500 Strong shaking. People will have difficulty to do things.
>=1000 Very strong shaking. People will have difficulty to stand.
>=2000 Extremely strong shaking. People will have difficulty to crawl on the ground.
>=3000 Catastrophic shaking. The wooden houses will crack.
